The National Library Game 📚💾

What does a national library actually do — and what does it take to keep a country's memory alive?

We rebuilt three national libraries as one explorable world: the British Library, the Dutch KB and the Swiss National Library — in two layers. By ☀️ day you walk the physical world: the reading rooms, the vaults deep underground, the satellite sites from Boston Spa to Lugano. Switch to 🌌 night and the same place becomes the digital world: the data archive glowing in the sky, virtual visitors, platforms and catalogues.

Switch countries and watch everything transform with the real numbers. Click the glowing dots — every discovery earns points, completing a whole library earns a bonus.
