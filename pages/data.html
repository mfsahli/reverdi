Data

ReVerDi is committed to open, reproducible research. We aim to publish the datasets behind our studies openly and make them publicly available.

Wherever possible, the raw data, inventories and code underpinning our results will be shared as open, well-documented datasets — so that others can reuse, verify and build on our work.

Our commitment
